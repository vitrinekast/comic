I've always found great

comfort in the cold

The cold feels safe. It's melancholic and calm.

The snow crunches shoftly. Under your boots, besides that it's quiet. Sounds are muffled under the blanket of snow, a frozen whisper in the stillness.

it gives me a glass like clarity that I only experience when the air is crisp. The hush of a snow covered landscape creates a comforting sense of tranquility and isolation
